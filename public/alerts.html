Questions
How often do alerts send?
Once per day, after the 8am CT data pull. If your rivers are fishing well on Tuesday, you get one email Tuesday morning. We only send when conditions are worth your attention: no noise.
What if my river doesn't have live data?
Alerts only fire when we have actual USGS gauge data for your river. Rivers without live sensors won't generate alerts: they'll still appear on the site but won't trigger notifications until data becomes available.
Can I change my rivers after subscribing?
Email chris@chrisizworski.com with your subscription email and your updated river list. We'll update it same day at no charge.
How do I cancel?
Click the unsubscribe link in any alert email, or email chris@chrisizworski.com. No questions asked. Your subscription won't auto-renew without action: you'll get a reminder email before the year ends.
Is this really $19/year flat?
Yes. One payment, one year, up to 8 rivers. No monthly fee, no per-alert charge, no upsell. The goal is to make this genuinely useful and affordable for anglers who fish Michigan regularly.
